About AgriLink

Our Mission

AgriLink is dedicated to connecting Nepal's hardworking farmers directly to conscious consumers, eliminating middlemen and creating a fair marketplace where farmers get paid fairly and customers receive the freshest produce.

Our Vision

To revolutionize Nepal's agricultural marketplace by creating a transparent, efficient, and fair platform that empowers farmers and provides consumers with access to the freshest, highest-quality produce from local sources.

Why We Started

We realized that Nepali farmers were struggling due to exploitation by middlemen, while consumers were paying inflated prices for produce that often wasn't fresh. AgriLink bridges this gap, creating value for everyone involved.

Our Values

  • Fairness - Ensuring fair prices for both farmers and customers
  • Freshness - Guaranteeing the freshest products through direct farm-to-home delivery
  • Transparency - Complete visibility into product origin and pricing
  • Community - Building strong relationships between farmers and consumers
  • Sustainability - Supporting environmentally responsible farming practices

How It Works

  1. Farmers register and list their products with photos, descriptions, and pricing
  2. Customers browse, search, and filter products to find what they need
  3. Orders are placed through our secure cart system
  4. Payments are processed through eSewa or Khalti
  5. Products are delivered directly from farm to home
  6. Customers can review products and provide feedback
